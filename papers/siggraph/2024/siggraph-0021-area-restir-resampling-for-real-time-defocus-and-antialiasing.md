# Area ReSTIR: Resampling for Real-Time Defocus and Antialiasing

## 摘要

Recent advancements in spatiotemporal reservoir resampling (ReSTIR) leverage sample reuse from neighbors to efficiently evaluate the path integral.&nbsp; Like rasterization, ReSTIR methods implicitly assume a pinhole camera and evaluate the light arriving at a pixel through a single predetermined subpixel location at a time (e.g., the pixel center).&nbsp; This prevents efficient path reuse in and near pixels with high-frequency details.
