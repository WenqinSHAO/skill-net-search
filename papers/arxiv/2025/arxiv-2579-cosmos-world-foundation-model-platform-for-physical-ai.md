# Cosmos World Foundation Model Platform for Physical AI

## 摘要

Physical AI needs to be trained digitally first. It needs a digital twin of itself, the policy model, and adigital twin of the world, the world model. In this paper, we present the Cosmos World Foundation ModelPlatform to help developers build customized world models for their Physical AI setups. We positiona world foundation model as a general-purpose world model that can be fine-tuned into customizedworld models for downstream applications. Our platform covers a video curation pipeline, pre-trainedworld foundation models, examples of post-training of pre-trained world foundation models, and videotokenizers. To help Physical AI builders solve the most critical problems of our society, we make ourplatform open-source and our models open-weight with permissive licenses available via NVIDIA Cosmos.
