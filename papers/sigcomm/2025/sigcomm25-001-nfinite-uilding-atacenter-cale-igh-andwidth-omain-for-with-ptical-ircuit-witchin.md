# InfiniteHBD: Building Datacenter-Scale High-Bandwidth Domain for LLM with Optical Circuit Switching Transceivers

**SIGCOMM 2025** | Chenchen Shou, Daxin Jiang, Guyue Liu, Hao Nie, Huaiyu Meng, Wenqing Lv, Yanbo Yu, Yelong Xu, Yibo Zhu, Yichen Shen, Yimin Jiang, Yu Zhou, Yuanwei Lu, Zhang Chen


## 摘要

Proposes InfiniteHBD, a transceiver-centric HBD architecture integrating Optical Circuit Switching (OCS) within each transceiver. 100 optical channels at 2 Gbps each, scaling to 800 Gbps with up to 50m reach.
